Our Story

DEVARO is the cleaner, more premium expression of the business behind the bakes.

Built in Auckland, DEVARO is designed to present the business with more focus and more confidence: one side for custom cakes, one side for daily bakery favourites, under a sharper black, gold and grey identity.

Focused

The site is intentionally simple. Instead of trying to show every possible business line at once, DEVARO concentrates on two experiences only: Cakery and Bakery.

Refined

The visual direction is minimal and premium, shaped by a black, gold and grey palette rather than a generic colourful bakery look.

Practical

Behind the presentation is a working bakery business in Auckland with the ability to keep evolving the product range, imagery and online ordering process over time.
